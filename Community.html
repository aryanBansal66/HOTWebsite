Community Guidelines

Welcome to House of Talentt!

House of Talentt (“the App”) is powered by VCS Organisation (“Entity”, “we”, “us” and “our”). The terms “you” and “your” refer to the user of this App.

We are committed to fostering a vibrant and positive community where creativity and collaboration can flourish. To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all users, we have established these Community Guidelines. These guidelines are designed to promote respectful interactions, protect user safety, and maintain the integrity of our platform.

Please read these guidelines in conjunction with our Content Policy and Terms and Conditions. Together, these documents outline the standards and rules that govern your use of our app and services. By participating in our community, you agree to adhere to these guidelines and acknowledge their integration with our Content Policy and Terms and Conditions. Your cooperation helps us create an inclusive environment where everyone can share their talents and ideas with confidence.

By adhering to these guidelines, users contribute to a supportive and creative community where everyone can enjoy and benefit from participating in video contests and sharing content.

1. Respectful Content

  • Be Kind and Respectful: Treat all users with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, and discriminatory content will not be tolerated.
  • No Bullying or Intimidation: Any form of bullying or intimidation is prohibited. Ensure that content does not target or demean individuals or groups.
  • Sensitive Topics: Approach sensitive topics with care. Avoid graphic or triggering content that might be harmful to others.

2. Content Standards

  • No Offensive Material: Avoid sharing content that includes explicit violence, hate speech, or adult content.
  • Intellectual Property: Ensure that all content, including music and visuals, complies with copyright laws. Only use content you own or have permission to use.
  • Misleading Information: Do not post misleading or false information. Ensure your content is accurate and trustworthy.

3. Contest Participation

  • Fair Play: Participate in contests fairly. Do not engage in cheating, including manipulating votes or using bots to gain an unfair advantage.
  • Original Content: Submit original content for contests. Do not plagiarize or reuse content from other sources unless explicitly allowed.
  • Compliance with Rules: Follow all contest-specific rules and guidelines. Ensure your submissions meet the requirements outlined for each contest.

4. Privacy and Security

  • Protect Personal Information: Do not share personal information (yours or others') in your videos or comments. Respect privacy.
  • Report Suspicious Activity: Report any suspicious activity or content that violates the Community Guidelines.

5. User Interaction

  • Constructive Feedback: Provide feedback that is constructive and helpful. Avoid negative or harmful comments.
  • Engagement Etiquette: Engage with other users' content respectfully. Avoid spamming, trolling, or engaging in inappropriate behaviour.

6. Legal Compliance

  • Follow the Law: Ensure all content complies with local, national, and international laws. Respect legal restrictions related to content sharing and adhere to the Content Policy and Terms and Conditions of the App.

7. Platform Integrity

  • No Spam or Misuse: Do not post spam, advertisements, or content intended solely for self-promotion without permission.
  • Account Security: Keep your account secure. Do not share your login details and report any suspicious activity immediately.

8. Consequences of Violations

  • Warnings and Actions: Violations of these guidelines may result in warnings, content removal, or account suspension depending on the severity and frequency of the infractions.
  • Appeal Process: Users have the right to appeal decisions made regarding guideline violations through our Grievance Redressal Mechanism.

9. Continuous Improvement

  • Feedback and Updates: We regularly review and update our community guidelines to ensure they remain relevant and effective. Your feedback is valuable in this process.

Thank you for being a part of House of Talentt and for contributing to a supportive and engaging community.
