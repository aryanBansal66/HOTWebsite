Contest Rules and Guidelines

Welcome to House of Talentt’s video-making contests! We’re excited to offer you the opportunity to showcase your creativity and potentially win fantastic rewards. By participating in these contests, you agree to follow the rules outlined below. Please ensure you are familiar with our Terms and Conditions and other applicable policies and guidelines of our App, as these govern your participation and use of the App.

1. Contest Participation

1.1 Contest Announcements:

Details about each video-making contest, including themes, deadlines, and entry requirements, will be published on dedicated contest pages or through notifications within the App. Participants are responsible for staying updated by regularly checking these sources.

1.2 Contest Entry:

  • Register for the contest.
  • Pay the entry fee using in-App Coins.
  • Submit their video through the App within the contest submission period.

1.3 Adherence to Contest Theme and Guidelines:

Entries must comply with the contest theme and all specific guidelines provided. Participants should review these guidelines thoroughly to ensure their entries are eligible.

2. Video Requirements

2.1 Originality:

Videos must be original works created by the user and should not infringe on any third-party rights, including copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property.

2.2 Content Specifications:

Videos must adhere to the specifications outlined in the contest guidelines, including but not limited to theme, format, length, and resolution.

3. Submission Period

Each contest will have a clearly communicated submission period within the App. Late entries will not be accepted.

4. Judging and Winner Selection

4.1 Judging Criteria:

Entries will be judged based on the number of likes they receive. The popularity of each entry, as determined by its total likes, will be a significant factor in the judging process.

4.2 Tie Situations:

In case of a tie, the prize will be equally divided among the tied users.

5. Prizes

5.1 Prize Details:

Prizes for each contest, including the amount and form, will be specified in the contest announcement. Prizes may be awarded in the form of Coins, which can be withdrawn according to the App’s Terms and Conditions and Transaction and Refund Policy.

6. Prize Withdrawal

6.1 Withdrawal Process:

Eligible winners can withdraw their cash prizes through the App’s authorized payment gateway. Withdrawals are subject to the payment gateway’s terms and conditions.

6.2 Verification Requirements:

Winners may need to provide additional verification information to process their withdrawal. Failure to provide the required information may result in prize forfeiture.

6.3 Withdrawal Fees:

Fees related to prize withdrawals may apply and will be deducted from the prize amount as specified by the payment gateway.

6.4 Tax Deductions:

Prize withdrawals are subject to applicable taxes under Indian law. Taxes will be deducted from the withdrawn amount as described in our Terms and Conditions.

6.5 Tax Certificate:

Tax Certificates will be issued within one month from the last date of filing TDS returns for each quarter.

6.6 Government Revisions:

In the event of future revisions by the Government of India to TDS rates or definitions of Net Winnings, TDS will be deducted according to the updated rates and definitions. Users are responsible for their own tax matters.

7. Governing Terms

Participation in video-making contests on the App is governed by our Terms and Conditions, Privacy Policy, and other applicable policies. By participating, you agree to abide by these rules as well as any amendments or updates.

8. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding these Contest Rules and Guidelines, please contact us through our Grievance Redressal Mechanism.

Acknowledgement

By participating in our video-making contests, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to these Contest Rules and Guidelines, as well as our Terms and Conditions and other applicable policies.
